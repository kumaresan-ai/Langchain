France is a country located in Western Europe. 
It is known for its rich history, art, cuisine, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

The capital of France is Paris. 
Paris is the largest city in France and is considered one of the most beautiful cities in the world. 
It is often called "The City of Light" because of its leading role during the Age of Enlightenment.

Other major cities in France include Lyon, Marseille, Toulouse, and Bordeaux.
